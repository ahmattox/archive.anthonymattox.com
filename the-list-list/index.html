The Project:

The List List is a collaborative list of lists. Here you can create this lists you've always wanted, and fill them with the most superlative things, or see what other people add.

How To:

Add new lists in the form just to the left and click on lists to view them. You can vote lists and items in lists up or down however you think is most appropriate. You can also click on the X to mark content as irrelevant or inappropriate. You can vote on each item once a day.

If you're adding lists, they should probably begin with a superlative (best, worst, most, etc.) so people can easily rank items within it.
